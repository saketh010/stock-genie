# Risk Framework

## Position Sizing Rules
1. **Maximum Allocation (High Conviction):** No single trade shall exceed **5%** of the total portfolio value, regardless of how strong the setup appears.
2. **Maximum Allocation (Medium Conviction):** Standard trades shall not exceed **2%** of the total portfolio value.
3. **Minimum Cash Reserve:** The portfolio must always maintain a minimum of **20%** cash at all times to capitalize on sudden market corrections.

## Diversification & Sector Limits
1. **Sector Concentration:** Never allocate more than **15%** of the total portfolio to a single sector (e.g., Financials, IT, Pharma). If a new signal triggers for a sector already at the limit, the trade must be rejected or an existing position must be trimmed first.
2. **Stock Bans:** 
   - No penny stocks (Price < ₹50).
   - No stocks with extremely low liquidity.
   - Avoid companies with active corporate governance red flags or pending severe regulatory actions.

## Stop Loss & Take Profit Mechanisms
1. **Hard Stop Loss:** Every position is subjected to an implicit trailing stop loss or a hard structural stop. Do not allow a single position to draw down more than **10%** from its entry price.
2. **Scaling Out:** For profitable positions, strongly consider scaling out (taking partial profits) when a stock hits its initial target or goes parabolic.
